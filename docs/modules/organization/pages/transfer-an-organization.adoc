= Transfer an organization
:navtitle: Transfer an organization

Learn how to transfer an organization to an admin in your organization. You can either transfer the organization and reassign yourself to an admin, or transfer the organization and remove yourself from the organization completely.

[NOTE]
Only the current owner can transfer an organization. If the current owner no longer works at your company, reach out to support@kobiton.com.

== Transfer and become an admin

In *Org Management*, select *Users*.

image:select-users-context.png[width=1000, alt="select Users tab in Org Management"]

Search for the user you want to transfer the organization to, then select their username.

image:select-user-name-context.png[width=1000,alt="Select the user's name in the list of users"]

Select *Make Organization Owner*.

image:make-org-owner-closeup.png[width=1000,alt="Click Make Org Owner to transfer the org ownership"]

Confirm ownership transfer. The current owner will become an admin.

image:confirmation-popup-closeup.png[width=500,alt="A confirmation pop-up asking of ownership transfer"]

== Transfer and disable your account

In *Org Management*, select *Users*.

image:$NEW-IMAGE$[width=, alt=""]

Search for your account, then select your username.

image:select-your-username-context.png[width=1000,alt="Select your username in the users list"]

Select *Disable*.

image:select-disable-closeup.png[width=1000,alt="click Disable to disable and transfer ownership"]

Confirm ownership transfer and disable your account:

image:transfer-and-disable-closeup.png[width=500,alt="Transfer and Disable account pop-up to confirm disable and transfer ownership"]
