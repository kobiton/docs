= Sign in to Kobiton
:navtitle: Sign in to Kobiton

Sign in to Kobiton Portal using your email address, username, or a linked Google or GitHub account. Available sign-in methods depend on your deployment type.

== Sign-in methods

Cloud deployments support the following sign-in methods:

* Email address or username with your Kobiton password
* Google account
* GitHub account

Standalone deployments support:

* Email address or username with your Kobiton password

Google and GitHub sign-in are not available for Standalone deployments.

[NOTE]
====
If your organization uses Single Sign-On (SSO), refer to
xref:organization:sso-authentication/about-sso-authentication.adoc[]
for SSO-specific sign-in instructions.
====

== Sign in with email or username

. Open the Kobiton Portal sign-in page.
. In the *Email or Username* field, enter your email address or Kobiton username. Your username is configured in xref:profile:manage-your-profile.adoc#_change_your_name[your profile].
. Enter your password.
. Select *Continue*.

[NOTE]
====
Cloud and Standalone deployments both support email address or username sign-in.
====

== Sign in with Google or GitHub

Google and GitHub sign-in are available for Cloud deployments only.

. Open the Cloud Portal sign-in page.
. Select *Log in with Google* or *Log in with GitHub*.
. Complete the provider sign-in and consent flow.

If the Google or GitHub email address is not associated with an existing Kobiton account, Kobiton creates a new account and organization automatically. The username is generated from the email address used during registration.

If you already have a Kobiton account, you can link your Google or GitHub account during sign-in.

If your account belongs to multiple organizations, you are prompted to select an organization after signing in.

The first time you link a Google or GitHub account, Kobiton imports your first name, last name, and profile picture from the provider. You can update these values later in xref:profile:manage-your-profile.adoc[your profile].

[NOTE]
====
Google and GitHub accounts cannot be linked to Standalone accounts.
====

== Reset your password

Password reset is available for Cloud Portal accounts.

. On the Cloud Portal sign-in page, select *Forgot Password?*.
. Enter your email address or username.
. Open the reset link sent to the email address associated with your account.
. Choose a new password.

[NOTE]
====
Standalone Portal does not support email-based password reset. If you cannot sign in to Standalone Portal, contact your organization administrator.
====
