= Sign in to Kobiton
:navtitle: Sign in to Kobiton

Sign in to Kobiton Portal with your email address, your username, or a linked Google or GitHub account. Available sign-in methods depend on which portal you use.

== Sign-in methods

Kobiton offers two portals, and they accept different sign-in methods.

Cloud Portal accepts:

* Email or username, with your Kobiton password
* Google account
* GitHub account

Standalone Portal accepts:

* Email or username, with your Kobiton password

Standalone Portal does not offer Google or GitHub sign-in. Standalone deployments often run in restricted or air-gapped environments without access to third-party identity providers.

[NOTE]
====
If your organization uses Single Sign-On (SSO), refer to
xref:organization:sso-authentication/about-sso-authentication.adoc[]
for the sign-in path that applies to your account.
====

== Sign in with email or username

. Open the sign-in page for your portal.
. In the *Email or Username* field, enter either your email address or
your Kobiton username. Your username is set in
xref:profile:manage-your-profile.adoc#_change_your_name[your profile].
. Enter your password.
. Select *Continue*.

[NOTE]
Both Cloud Portal and Standalone Portal accept email or username in the same field. Email format is no longer required.

== Sign in with Google or GitHub

Google and GitHub sign-in are available on Cloud Portal only.

. Open the Cloud Portal sign-in page.
. Select *Log in with Google* or *Log in with GitHub*.
. Complete the provider's consent screen and authorize Kobiton.

What happens next depends on whether your provider email is already
associated with a Kobiton account:

[cols="1,3"]
|===
| Account state | Result

| No Kobiton account exists for the provider email
| A new Kobiton account and organization are created, then you are redirected to the Device List.

| Provider email matches an existing Kobiton account, no provider linked
| An account-linking prompt appears. Confirm to link your provider account to the existing Kobiton account.

| Provider account is already linked, and you belong to one organization
| You are signed in and redirected to the Device List.

| Provider account is already linked, and you belong to multiple organizations
| You are prompted to choose an organization before continuing.
|===

The first time you link a Google or GitHub account, your first name, last name, and profile picture are populated from the provider. You can update these values later from xref:profile:manage-your-profile.adoc[your profile].

[NOTE]
Linking a Google or GitHub account to an Enterprise SSO account is not supported.

== Reset your password

Password reset by email is available on Cloud Portal. Standalone Portal does not support email-based password reset.

On Cloud Portal:

. On the sign-in page, select *Forgot Password?*.
. Enter your email address or username.
. Open the reset link in the email sent to the address associated with your account.
. Choose a new password.

[NOTE]
If you sign in to Standalone Portal and do not remember your password, contact your organization administrator.
