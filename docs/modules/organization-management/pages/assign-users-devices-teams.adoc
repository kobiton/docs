= Assign users/ devices to Teams
:navtitle: Assign users/ devices to Teams

== Assign/ Remove users from Teams
=== Assign users
1. On *Org Management*, select the *Teams* tab.

2. Navigate to the Teams Details site by clicking on the Team name.
+
image::organization-management:assign-users-context.png[width=1000,alt="Click on the team name to direct to Teams Details site"]

3. Click the *Users* tab.
+
image::organization-management:assign-users-closeup.png[width=400,alt="Click the Users tab"]

4. Click *Add new user*.
+
image::organization-management:assign-users-context1.png[width=1000,alt="Click Add new user tab"]
5. Toggle one role amongst *Team Owner*, *Team Admin* Or *Team Member* to select the role you want to assign the user to.


6. Use search box or select users from the Unassigned Users drop-down list to add them to the Team.

7. Click *Update* to close the modal.
+
image::organization-management:assign-users-closeup1.png[width=400,alt="Assign a user to a Team Role"]

8. Click *Save*.

=== Remove users

1. On the Team Details site, click the *Users* tab.

2. Click the (-) icon to remove users from the team.
+
image::organization-management:assign-users-closeup2.png[width=1000,alt="Remove a user from a role"]

3. Click *Save*.

== Assign/ Remove devices from Teams

1. On the Team Details site, click the *Devices* tab.
+
image::organization-management:assign-devices-closeup.png[width=400,alt="Click Devices tab in the Team Details site"]

2. To filter online/ offline devices, tick the *Online/ Offline* button. You can also filter *Public/ Private & Local/ All devices* by selecting one option from the drop-down list.
+
image::organization-management:assign-devices-closeup2.png[width=400,alt="Select types of devices"]

3. In the list of *Unassigned Devices*, click (+) icon to add a device.
+
image::organization-management:assign-devices-context2.png[width=1000,alt="Click (+) to add devices"]
+
To remove a device, click (-) from the *Assigned Devices* list.
+
image::organization-management:assign-devices-context4.png[width=1000,alt="Click (-) to remove devices"]

4. Click the *Assign All* button to assign all devices in the current *Unassigned Devices* list. Click the *Unassign All* button to remove all devices from the *Assigned Devices* list.

5. Click *Save*.

NOTE: As a device can be assigned to multiple teams, it may cause conflicts regarding Cleanup Policies amongst Teams. For example, a Team may set a No Cleanup policy while another Team set a Thorough policy to the same device.
